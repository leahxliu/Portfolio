AWARE App

November 2020

This is a mock-up app design of 3 different artists from AWARE Women Artists. The focus is on typography and some interaction design.

I designed my app very minimally and with few colors because I wanted the interface to be very calm and static. The calm and static design reflects my own wishes for web browsing on phones; I dislike web browsing on my phone due to the overwhelming amount of popups and ads going on.
