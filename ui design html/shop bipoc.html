Shop BIPOC

July 2020

This was my final project for the UI/UX Bootcamp Course I took. In the midst of BLM protests and a devastating pandemic, my team and I developed a mock-up site to support BIPOC owned businesses. The concept is relatively simple; it is a tool to help users find and support local BIPOC owned businesses. We also included a support tab to direct interested users to donation links and other helpful links.

I created initial and mid-fidelity prototypes and also helped ideate throught the proccess. I also helped code the final page in our prototype.

Future development would include building out more of the site tabs from the navigation bar and cleaning up some of the grpahics.
