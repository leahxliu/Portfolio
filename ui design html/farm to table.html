Farm to Table Redesign

March 2020

This was a group project to redesign the Farm to Table website. This was the beginning of the COVID-19 pandemic, and Farm to Table had not updated it's site yet for placing orders and deliveries.

First, the group performed a heuristic evaluation of Farm to Table's website at the time and determined what aspects of the site we liked and wanted to include in our own design. We also examined pain points to change or redo. Since Farm to Table is an E-Commerce site, my group modelled the interface after HEB's model. In order to stay true to Farm to Table, my group styled and included farmer profiles and the mission statement from the original site. Finally, to streamline processes for returning customers, the team included a login option and resurfaced the checkout page. I developed different style elements such as fonts and colors and layouts for some of the pages.
