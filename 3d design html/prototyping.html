Packaging Prototyping

November 2020

This is a project to explore different packaging options for a honey and tea kit. I Explored many different forms through sketch and prototyping with cardboard.

First I looked through pinterest for packaing inspiration and found both unique and most common forms for kit and packaing design. I then started sketching out my own ideas. After sketching I began prototyping at a small scale with cardboard and paper. I narrowed those down and concentrated on two forms, looking at the pros and cons of both. At the end, I decided on a relatively simple and common form which displays the product clearly and utilizes space well.
