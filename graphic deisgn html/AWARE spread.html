AWARE Magazine Spread

November 2020

For this project, I was tasked to create a magazine spread for an article from AWARE Women Artists. My article is about women artists and psychiatry. I incorporated the image from the article in the backround of the spread and manipulated the text to reflect the article's content. I made sure that the title text was still legible since it serves an important purpose. I used a two column system to improve readability and also made sure to include relevant information (like the artist, author, and source).

I have some greivances with this, but some of the elements were out of my control, such as the image used. I would've preffered a more impactful image to fill the space.
