Zongzi Poster

May 2021

Zongzi is a traditional Chinese dish eaten to celebrate the Dragon Boat Festival. I combined illustration, photography, and text to create this poster. I used illustration to try to embody the richness of this food; it is very savory and aromatic. I went through many iterations of this poster in order to land on this final product.

I imagine this poster living in a restauraunt or as a letter size info-graphic that comes with a cookbook or something.
