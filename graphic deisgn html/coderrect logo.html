Coderrect Logo

August 2020

I was comissioned to create a logo for a company developing a multi-threaded race detection software called Coderrect. They wanted to have a modern and sleek design, reflecting a reliable and high-tech software.

I spent a few days making a multitude of sketches for potential directions. Then I used Adobe Illustrator to refine the most viable logos. I narrowed down my designs and presented them to my employer.

Moving forward, we began considering font options. They wanted something compact and tech related due to the vertical nature of the logomark. I altered an existing font and made the font color grey to give emphasis to the logomark.

Finally, we chose colors for the logo. Following design trends, I presented an array of both gradient and single color options. Taking from their original logo, we chose a deep green color. As a final deliverable, I presented different versions of the logo (black and white, vertical, horizonatl, etc.),

This is their site: https://coderrect.com/.
