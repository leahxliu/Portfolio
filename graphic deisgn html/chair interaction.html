Chair Interaction

October 2019

I was instructed to create a poster of a place or object and people's interactions with it. I chose a particularly charming chair in a starbucks in my hometown. I sat in the starbucks for a few hours and documented movement around the store and around the chairs there.

I sketched many thumbnail sketches to get the layout of the information just right and practiced different shading styles. I think there could be more spacing between some of the elements, but I'm really happy with the way it turned out despite a few mistakes while putting down the marker.
