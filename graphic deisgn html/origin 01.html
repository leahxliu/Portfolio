Origin 01 Font

July 2023

Inspired by a style I frequently used in my sketchbook, I made this geometric sans serif font. I use this font to express strong thoughts and feelings in my sketchbook and wanted to reflect that with the strong geometry and structure. Many characters have an adjustable width to add interest and fill space. I included some experiments and examples of how it could be used. Photos taken by me.
