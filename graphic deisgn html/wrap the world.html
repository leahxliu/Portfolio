Wrap the World

December 2022

Wrap the World is a collaborative interactive 50 page zine featuring different wrapped foods from around the world. It highlights wrapped foods as an experience while also highlighting foods of other cultures. Of course, this zine does not cover all or even most of the wrapped foods from around the world.

The zine has 3 main sections; celebration food, day to day foods, and graphs. In the celebration food section, each food has a description of the food, an interactive foldable with ingredients, and a step by step folding guide. The day to day foods section is simpler and includes a brief description of the wrapped food and illustrations of the food and its ingredients. The graph portion features the foods on a variety of graphs.
