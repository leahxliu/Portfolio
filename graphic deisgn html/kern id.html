Kern Conference Identity

December 2021

Kern Conference is a fictitious type design conference which I designed an identity system for. The theme of the conference is "space," a future-facing and technological concept and a play on the meaning of "kern". I liked the high contrast and bright colors of space and the waviness of galaxies, so I took inspiration from those concepts to create the logo. After designing the logo, I used elements of it to shape the brand guidelines. Other brand guidelines like Discord's and Twitch's inspired my own.
