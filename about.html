ABOUT ME

Hello! I'm a Texan artist and designer. With a background in traditional art and illustration, my main body of work spreads across graphic design, UI design, and packaging design. Throughout these different avenues, I strive to create thought-provoking, awe-inspiring, and purposeful designs that can educate and inspire my audience. I'm always on the lookout for inspiring and clever designs I can use in my own creations. Outside of my work, I spend my time on the prowl for good food, making art, and playing video games.
