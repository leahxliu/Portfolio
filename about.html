ABOUT ME

Howdy! I'm a Texan designer and artist. With a background in traditional art, my main body of work spreads across layout design, branding/identity design, and web design. Throughout these different avenues, I strive to create thought-provoking, awe-inspiring, and purposeful designs that can educate and inspire my audience.

I'm always on the lookout for eye catching and innovative designs to inspire my own work. Outside of designing, I spend my time pursuing passions like cooking, making art, and playing games.
