ABOUT ME

Hello! I am a Texan artist and designer. I have practiced traditional art for much of my life and after growing bored of realism, I turned my attention to design. After a break from mandatory art-making in high school, I found that I am still interested in illustration, but I find design much more enticing and stimulating. I fell in love with game and movie art and I want to create designs or illustrations in that kind of field someday.

I am currently studying for a B.F.A. in Design and for an Elements of Computing Certificate at the University of Austin.
